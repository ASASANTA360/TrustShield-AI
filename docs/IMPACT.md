# TRUSTSHIELD AI Impact

## Our Mission

To create a safer digital world by providing open, intelligent, and accessible trust infrastructure.

---

## Problems We Address

- Identity fraud
- Fake accounts
- Digital scams
- Low trust in online transactions

---

## Expected Impact

### Individuals

- Safer digital interactions
- Better protection from fraud

### Businesses

- Improved KYC processes
- Reduced financial risks

### Communities

- Increased digital trust
- More secure online ecosystems

---

## Why Open Source?

Open-source technology enables global collaboration, transparency, and accessibility.

TRUSTSHIELD AI aims to become a public digital trust infrastructure that anyone can improve and use.
